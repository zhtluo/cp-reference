Compute the prefix sum of multiplicative functions.

Usage:
\begin{compactenum}
	\item Modify \mintinline{cpp}|pre_pow| to compute the sum of powers.
	\item Modify \mintinline{cpp}|pfunc| to compute $f(p)$ with a prime $p$.
	\item Modify \mintinline{cpp}|cfunc| to compute $f(px)$ with $f(x)=k$ and $p|x$.
	\item Modify \mintinline{cpp}|assemble| to store $f(x_i)$ in \mintinline{cpp}|funca[i]| with $x_i^k$ equal to \mintinline{cpp}|powa[k][i]| and \mintinline{cpp}|funcb[i]| with $x_i^k$ equal to \mintinline{cpp}|powb[k][i]|.
	\item Execute \mintinline{cpp}|solve| and profit.
\end{compactenum}
